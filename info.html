ConveX3D Earth

ConveX3D Earth is an educational program whose goal is to spread geographical knowledge to anyone. It offers an alphabetical list of all countries in the world, whose locations can be pinpointed on a three-dimensional world map. In addition to that, further information regarding those countries can be retrieved.

Three-dimensional world map

The rich and detailed three-dimensional world map can be used for browsing and exploring the topographical characteristics of the earth. On top of that country boundaries can be displayed, giving an insight view on the territorial proportions of the earth.

The navigation is done by using only the mouse. By grabbing the earth with the left mouse button while dragging it, a rotational movement can be achieved. Zooming in and out is possible using the mouse wheel. Alternatively the right mouse button can be held while moving the mouse up or down. Resetting the view to its original state can be achieved by pushing the button Reset view. In a similar fashion political country boundaries can be toggled by pushing the button Country boundaries.

Country list and information

The country list, which is located on the bottom of the page, consists of a total of 250 countries. Each country is displayed with its capital and the continent it is part of. By clicking on a country name, additional information to that specific country will be displayed on the top right hand corner of the page.
